<p><b>Halalgoogling</b> is an Islamic internet search engine aimed at <a href="/wiki/Muslim" title="Muslim">Muslim</a> users, during the holy month of <a href="/wiki/Ramadan" title="Ramadan">Ramadan</a>, to block <a href="/wiki/Haram" title="Haram">haram</a> content, according to the <a href="/wiki/Sharia_law" class="mw-redirect" title="Sharia law">Sharia law</a>. It collects results from other <a href="/wiki/Web_search_engine" title="Web search engine">web search engine</a>s like <a href="/wiki/Google_Search" title="Google Search">Google</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bing_(search_engine)" title="Bing (search engine)">Bing</a> and presents only the <a href="/wiki/Halal" title="Halal">halal</a> results to its users.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><br />
