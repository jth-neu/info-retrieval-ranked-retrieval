<p><b>Mugurdy</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Visual_search_engine" title="Visual search engine">visual search engine</a> that launched its public beta in July 2009. In addition to textual results of search queries it shows a screenshot of each webpage. Mugurdy, created by Software du Jour, is the first company to enter the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Guinness_Enterprise_Centre&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Guinness Enterprise Centre (page does not exist)">Guinness Enterprise Centre</a>’s <a href="/wiki/Microsoft_BizSpark" title="Microsoft BizSpark">Microsoft BizSpark</a> cluster.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
