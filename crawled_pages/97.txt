<p><b>EXALEAD</b> <span class="nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups noexcerpt"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/English" title="Help:IPA/English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/ɛ/: &#39;e&#39; in &#39;dress&#39;">ɛ</span><span title="/ɡ/: &#39;g&#39; in &#39;guy&#39;">ɡ</span><span title="/ˈ/: primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="&#39;z&#39; in &#39;zoom&#39;">z</span><span title="/æ/: &#39;a&#39; in &#39;bad&#39;">æ</span><span title="&#39;l&#39; in &#39;lie&#39;">l</span><span title="/iː/: &#39;ee&#39; in &#39;fleece&#39;">iː</span><span title="&#39;d&#39; in &#39;dye&#39;">d</span></span>/</a></span></span> is a software company, created in 2000, that provided search platforms and <a href="/wiki/Search-based_application" title="Search-based application">search-based applications</a> (SBA) <sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> for consumer and business users. The company is headquartered in Paris, France, and is a subsidiary of <a href="/wiki/Dassault_Syst%C3%A8mes" title="Dassault Systèmes">Dassault Systèmes</a> <sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> (<small>French pronunciation:&#160;&#8203;</small><span title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" class="IPA"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/French" title="Help:IPA/French">[daˈso]</a></span>).
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="CloudView">CloudView</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Exalead&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: CloudView">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The company's CloudView product is search and information access software used for both online and enterprise search-based applications as well as <a href="/wiki/Enterprise_search" title="Enterprise search">enterprise search</a>. CloudView combines Web-scale semantic technologies, rapid drag-and-drop application development and hybrid quantitative/qualitative analytics to deliver a consumer-style information experience to mission-critical business processes. In the case of structured data, the SBA index replaces a traditional <a href="/wiki/Relational_database" title="Relational database">relational database</a> structure as the primary vehicle for information access and reporting.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Exalabs">Exalabs</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Exalead&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Exalabs">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The CloudView product is also the platform for Exalead's public Web <a href="/wiki/Search_engine" class="mw-redirect" title="Search engine">search engine</a>,  which was designed to apply semantic processing and <a href="/wiki/Faceted_search" title="Faceted search">faceted navigation</a> to Web data volumes and usage. Exalead also operates an online laboratory which uses the Web as a medium for developing applied technologies for business.
</p><p>Many of Exalabs projects are developed in conjunction with Exalead's partners in the <a href="/wiki/Quaero" title="Quaero">Quaero</a> <sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> project.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Exalead&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Exalead was founded in 2000 by <a href="/wiki/Fran%C3%A7ois_Bourdoncle" title="François Bourdoncle">François Bourdoncle</a> and Patrice Bertin (both of whom were involved in the development of the <a href="/wiki/Alta_Vista" class="mw-redirect" title="Alta Vista">Alta Vista</a> search engine), and began commercializing its products in 2005. Exalead employed approximately 150 people.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style/Dates_and_numbers#Chronological_items" title="Wikipedia:Manual of Style/Dates and numbers"><span title="The time period mentioned near this tag is ambiguous. (September 2016)">when?</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p><p>On 9 June 2010, <a href="/wiki/Dassault_Syst%C3%A8mes" title="Dassault Systèmes">Dassault Systèmes</a> acquired Exalead for a total amount of 135 million Euros. 
Since then, many startups have sprung from Exalead like Dataiku founded by Florian Douetteau, former vice president and then CEO of <a href="/wiki/Dataiku" title="Dataiku">Dataiku</a>, a French analytics software editor.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
