<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">List of volcanoes in the United Kingdom</h1>			<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">

</p>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Lists_of_volcanoes" title="Lists of volcanoes">Lists of volcanoes</a></div>
<p>There are no active volcanoes in the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, but there are a few active volcanoes in some <a href="/wiki/British_Overseas_Territories" title="British Overseas Territories">British Overseas Territories</a>. <a href="/wiki/Queen_Mary%27s_Peak" title="Queen Mary&#39;s Peak">Queen Mary's Peak</a> in <a href="/wiki/Tristan_da_Cunha" title="Tristan da Cunha">Tristan da Cunha</a> erupted in 1961-2. The <a href="/wiki/Soufri%C3%A8re_Hills" title="Soufrière Hills">Soufrière Hills</a> volcano on the Caribbean island of <a href="/wiki/Montserrat" title="Montserrat">Montserrat</a> erupted three times between 1995 and 2013. <a href="/wiki/Mount_Belinda" title="Mount Belinda">Mount Belinda</a> in the British Overseas Territory of <a href="/wiki/South_Georgia_and_the_South_Sandwich_Islands" title="South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands">South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands</a>, erupted between 2001 and 2007. <a href="/wiki/Mount_Michael" title="Mount Michael">Mount Michael</a>, also in South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands, was active in 2005; its 700-metre-diameter (2,300&#160;ft) summit crater is thought to possibly contain an active <a href="/wiki/Lava_lake" title="Lava lake">lava lake</a>, one of only a handful in the world.
</p><p>Below is a list of extinct <a href="/wiki/Volcano" title="Volcano">volcanoes</a> in the United Kingdom.
