<p><b>ACM Fellowship</b> is an award and <a href="/wiki/Fellow" title="Fellow">fellowship</a> that recognises outstanding members of the <a href="/wiki/Association_for_Computing_Machinery" title="Association for Computing Machinery">Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-facm_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-facm-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The title of ACM <a href="/wiki/Fellow" title="Fellow">Fellow</a> indicates excellence, as evidenced by technical, professional and leadership contributions that:
</p>
<ul><li>advance computing</li>
<li>promote the free exchange of ideas</li>
<li>advance the objectives of ACM</li></ul>
<p>At most 1% of the ACM membership may be elected as Fellows.<sup id="cite_ref-facm_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-facm-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Fellows">Fellows</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=ACM_Fellow&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Fellows">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>New fellows are elected annually since 1993.<sup id="cite_ref-facm_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-facm-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> As of  2016<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=ACM_Fellow&amp;action=edit">&#91;update&#93;</a></sup> examples of ACM Fellows include <a href="/wiki/Vint_Cerf" title="Vint Cerf">Vint Cerf</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anne_Condon" title="Anne Condon">Anne Condon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Serge_Abiteboul" title="Serge Abiteboul">Serge Abiteboul</a>. <sup id="cite_ref-fellowsaz_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fellowsaz-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> See also the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Fellows_of_the_Association_for_Computing_Machinery" title="List of Fellows of the Association for Computing Machinery">List of Fellows of the Association for Computing Machinery</a> and <a href="/wiki/Category:Fellows_of_the_Association_for_Computing_Machinery" title="Category:Fellows of the Association for Computing Machinery">Category:Fellows of the Association for Computing Machinery</a> for more examples. Some fellows use the <a href="/wiki/Post-nominal_letters" title="Post-nominal letters">post-nominal letters</a> <a href="/wiki/FACM" class="mw-redirect" title="FACM">FACM</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources.
