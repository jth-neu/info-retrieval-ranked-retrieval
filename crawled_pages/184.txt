<p><b>Seznam.cz</b> (or just <i>Seznam</i>, which means <i>directory</i> in English) is a <a href="/wiki/Web_portal" title="Web portal">web portal</a> and <a href="/wiki/Search_engine" class="mw-redirect" title="Search engine">search engine</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Czech_Republic" title="Czech Republic">Czech Republic</a>. Founded in 1996 by <a href="/wiki/Ivo_Luka%C4%8Dovi%C4%8D" title="Ivo Lukačovič">Ivo Lukačovič</a> in <a href="/wiki/Prague" title="Prague">Prague</a> as the first web portal in the Czech Republic. Seznam started with a <a href="/wiki/Search_engine" class="mw-redirect" title="Search engine">search engine</a> and an internet version of <a href="/wiki/Yellow_pages" title="Yellow pages">yellow pages</a>. Today, Seznam runs more than 15 different web services and associated brands. Seznam had more than 6 million real users per month at the end of 2014.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Among the most popular services, according to NetMonitor, are its homepage seznam.cz, email.cz, search.seznam.cz and its yellow pages firmy.cz.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (November 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p><p>In the Czech market, Seznam.cz was until 2008 in competition with the portals <a href="/w/index.php?title=Centrum.cz&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Centrum.cz (page does not exist)">Centrum.cz</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Atlas.cz&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Atlas.cz (page does not exist)">Atlas.cz</a>. These two portals, despite merging in 2008, no longer appear likely to overcome Seznam in the near future. Seznam's biggest competitor now appears to be global <a href="/wiki/Google" title="Google">Google</a>, especially in the area of full-text search.
</p><p>As of May 2012, according to Toplist results, Seznam was the second <a href="/wiki/Internet_search" class="mw-redirect" title="Internet search">internet search</a> engine in the Czech Republic (42.84%) with Google in the top spot (54.69%).<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>. By August 2014, still according to Toplist's statistics, Seznam's share of searches had further eroded to 38%.
</p><p>Seznam, like four other companies in the world, was<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> for a long time the number one search engine in its region. Other such search engines are <a href="/wiki/Baidu" title="Baidu">Baidu</a> (<a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>), <a href="/wiki/Naver" title="Naver">Naver</a> (<a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>), <a href="/wiki/Yahoo_Japan" class="mw-redirect" title="Yahoo Japan">Yahoo Japan</a> (<a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>) and <a href="/wiki/Yandex" title="Yandex">Yandex</a> (<a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Since 2011, Seznam has used as its slogan a Czech rhyme <i>Seznam - najdu tam, co hledám</i> (<i>Seznam - I find there, what I search for</i>). Prior to this its slogan was <i>Seznam - najdu tam, co neznám</i> (<i>Seznam - I find there, what I don't know</i>).
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Seznam.cz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Founded by <a href="/wiki/Ivo_Luka%C4%8Dovi%C4%8D" title="Ivo Lukačovič">Ivo Lukačovič</a> in 1996, Seznam began as the first internet <a href="/wiki/Yellow_pages" title="Yellow pages">yellow pages</a> and <a href="/wiki/Search_engine" class="mw-redirect" title="Search engine">search engine</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Czech_Republic" title="Czech Republic">Czech Republic</a>. Ivo started his business with 50,000 <a href="/wiki/CZK" class="mw-redirect" title="CZK">CZK</a> he had saved up.  In 1998, Seznam added email to its portfolio, with email accounts running on the <i>@seznam.cz</i> domain.  Since then, more brands and services have come on-line.  Since 2001, Seznam has included financial, social and political news, TV programs, weather forecasts, dictionaries, maps and community services.
</p><p>In 2000, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Spray_International&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Spray International (page does not exist)">Spray International</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Swedish</a> web portal company, invested in Seznam.  At that time Seznam became a <a href="/wiki/Joint_stock_company" class="mw-redirect" title="Joint stock company">joint stock company</a>.  Until 2000, Ivo Lukačovič was self-employed and Seznam was his own sole business.  With the investment, it is estimated that Spray International acquired around 30% share of Seznam.  Exact numbers are not known, as Lukačovič and Seznam officials have never released any details about the transaction.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>  What has been confirmed, though, is that Ivo Lukačovič retained at least 70% of Seznam stock after the investment.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p><p>Spray acquired a stake in Seznam with the intention of making an <a href="/wiki/Initial_Public_Offering" class="mw-redirect" title="Initial Public Offering">IPO</a>; however, Spray never reached that goal.  After the <a href="/wiki/Dot-com_bubble" title="Dot-com bubble">dot-com bubble</a> burst in 2000, Spray abandoned the idea.  There are rumors about Spray defaulting on its obligations to Ivo Lukačovič by not making an IPO.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> In 2003, Ivo Lukačovič increased the base capital of Seznam without Spray and became 70% owner of Seznam.  Since 2003, Ivo Lukačovič has remained the controlling shareholder of Seznam.
</p><p>Until 2003, Seznam had been using a fulltext engine from the Czech company Jyxo; however, in 2005, Seznam replaced that search engine with its own technology.  Also in 2005, Seznam acquired the Czech firm ATC which was, among other ventures, running the email server Email.cz.  Email services Email.cz and Seznam.cz have now merged.
</p><p>In 2006, Seznam moved its headquarters from <a href="/wiki/Prague" title="Prague">Prague</a> <a href="/wiki/Ko%C5%A1%C3%AD%C5%99e" title="Košíře">Košíře</a> to a more prominent location in the <a href="/wiki/And%C4%9Bl_(neighborhood)" title="Anděl (neighborhood)">Prague Anděl</a>.  That year, Ivo Lukačovič gave up the day-to-day business direction of Seznam, and Pavel Zima became CEO. In the Fall of 2006, Seznam acquired all email users from another web portal, "Czech On Line", with the domain <i>@post.cz</i>.
</p><p>Seznam bought 50% of Global Inspiration, a Czech company running a video portal Stream.cz, similar to the global <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTube</a> in 2007.  <a href="/wiki/Lycos_Europe" title="Lycos Europe">Lycos Europe</a> (a subsequent incarnation of Spray International) sold its 30% share in Seznam to <a href="/wiki/Tiger_Management" title="Tiger Management">Tiger Holding</a> and Miura International.  Lycos received more than 1.7 billion <a href="/wiki/CZK" class="mw-redirect" title="CZK">CZK</a> from this transaction.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (November 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> As of 2015, Miura International S.a.r.l. had 5,6% of shares, Tiger holding four S.a.r.l. had 24,4% of shares and Helifreak limited had 70% of shares.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2008, the Czech media reported on alleged meetings between Seznam and <a href="/wiki/Goldman_Sachs" title="Goldman Sachs">Goldman Sachs</a> representatives to find a buyer for Seznam. Ivo Lukačovič and Seznam representatives denied this. Such speculation ended with the start of the <a href="/wiki/Financial_crisis_of_2007%E2%80%932010" class="mw-redirect" title="Financial crisis of 2007–2010">financial crisis</a> in <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a> in late 2008.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Assessment">Assessment</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Seznam.cz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Assessment">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Seznam" class="mw-redirect" title="Seznam">Seznam</a> uses its own ranking scale for web pages, called S-Rank.  It is a similar to <a href="/wiki/PageRank" title="PageRank">PageRank</a> with a range from 0 to 10.
</p>
