<p><b>Empas</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">hangul</a>: 엠파스) was one of the popular total <a href="/wiki/Internet" title="Internet">internet</a> <a href="/wiki/Web_search_engine" title="Web search engine">search</a> tools and <a href="/wiki/Web_portal" title="Web portal">web portal</a> sites in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-KT201505129_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KT201505129-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The service was launched in 1998 by Knowledge Plant Corporation (<span lang="ko" title="Korean language text">지식발전소</span>), which changed its name to Empas Corporation in 2004. The name <i>Empas</i> is a combination of <i>e-media</i> and <i>compass</i>.
</p><p>Before its merger with <a href="/wiki/Nate_(web_portal)" title="Nate (web portal)">Nate</a> in 2009, Empas was one of South Korea's most popular web search engines, and competed with <a href="/wiki/Yahoo!_Korea" title="Yahoo! Korea">Yahoo! Korea</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daum_Communications" class="mw-redirect" title="Daum Communications">Daum</a>, Nate, and <a href="/wiki/Naver" title="Naver">Naver</a>. Empas was the second most popular web portal in the country from 2000 to 2001, by unique page view, behind <a href="/wiki/Yahoo!_Korea" title="Yahoo! Korea">Yahoo! Korea</a>. Since the dominance of <a href="/wiki/Naver" title="Naver">Naver</a> started in 2003, however, the market share of Empas declined, and by late 2005, it had fallen into the fifth place amongst the South Korean web portals. In 2006, aiming to check its main competition Naver's rapid expansion, SK Communications (which owns South Korea's popular social networking website <a href="/wiki/Cyworld" title="Cyworld">Cyworld</a>) acquired Empas on 19 October 2006.
</p><p>With the reorganisation of SK Communications's business fields, Empas was merged with Nate.com, which was the original web portal of SK Communications, and became Nate on 1 March 2009.<sup id="cite_ref-KT201505129_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KT201505129-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
