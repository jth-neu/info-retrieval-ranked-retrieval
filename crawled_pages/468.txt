<p>The <b>Sheffield Urban Area</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Conurbation" title="Conurbation">conurbation</a> in the North of England with a population of 685,368 according to the 2011 census.<sup id="cite_ref-BUA_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BUA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This was a 7% increase on the 2001 population of 640,720<sup id="cite_ref-excelsheet_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-excelsheet-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> making it the 8th largest <a href="/wiki/Conurbation" title="Conurbation">conurbation</a> in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a> and <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>'s 6th largest.  Named the Sheffield Urban Area by the <a href="/wiki/Office_for_National_Statistics" title="Office for National Statistics">Office for National Statistics</a>, it must not be confused with the <a href="/wiki/Sheffield_City_Region" class="mw-redirect" title="Sheffield City Region">Sheffield City Region</a>, a non-government term, although often used, particularly by the business community. In 2001 the population density of the urban area was 3,949.2 people per km<sup>2</sup> <sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> by 2011 this had increased slightly to 4,092 people per km<sup>2</sup>.<sup id="cite_ref-BUA_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BUA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The city of <a href="/wiki/Sheffield" title="Sheffield">Sheffield</a> contributes just over two thirds of the population of the whole conurbation.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Nearby_Places">Nearby Places</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sheffield_urban_area&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Nearby Places">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Settlements very close to the conurbation but not included in the official statistics include <a href="/w/index.php?title=Warncliffe_Side&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Warncliffe Side (page does not exist)">Warncliffe Side</a> and <a href="/wiki/Oughtibridge" title="Oughtibridge">Oughtibridge</a>, which are separated from <a href="/wiki/Hillsborough,_South_Yorkshire" class="mw-redirect" title="Hillsborough, South Yorkshire">Hillsborough</a> by <a href="/wiki/Worrall" title="Worrall">Worrall</a> and a half-mile stretch of the <a href="/wiki/A6102_road" class="mw-redirect" title="A6102 road">A6102</a> Middlewood Road North and the towns of <a href="/wiki/Wath_upon_Dearne" class="mw-redirect" title="Wath upon Dearne">Wath upon Dearne</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brampton_Bierlow" title="Brampton Bierlow">Brampton Bierlow</a>, <a href="/wiki/Swinton,_South_Yorkshire" title="Swinton, South Yorkshire">Swinton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mexborough" title="Mexborough">Mexborough</a>, <a href="/wiki/Darfield,_South_Yorkshire" title="Darfield, South Yorkshire">Darfield</a> and <a href="/wiki/Conisbrough" title="Conisbrough">Conisbrough</a> which are part of the <a href="/wiki/Dearne_Valley" title="Dearne Valley">Dearne Valley</a> Urban Area and are separated from Rawmarsh by the Wentworth Road, with the <a href="/wiki/A633_road" class="mw-redirect" title="A633 road">A633</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dearne_Valley_Line" class="mw-redirect" title="Dearne Valley Line">Dearne Valley</a> railway lines.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><br />
Slightly further afield, <a href="/wiki/Dronfield" title="Dronfield">Dronfield</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Derbyshire" title="Derbyshire">Derbyshire</a>, is around half a mile away from the <a href="/wiki/Greenhill,_Sheffield" class="mw-redirect" title="Greenhill, Sheffield">Greenhill</a> area of the city along the <a href="/wiki/A61_road" title="A61 road">A61</a> and B6158.
</p>
