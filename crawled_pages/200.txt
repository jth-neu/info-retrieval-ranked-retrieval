<p><b>Soso</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>&#58; <span lang="zh">搜搜</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>&#58; <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Sōusōu</span></i>) was a Chinese <a href="/wiki/Search_engine" class="mw-redirect" title="Search engine">search engine</a> owned by <a href="/wiki/Tencent_Holdings_Limited" class="mw-redirect" title="Tencent Holdings Limited">Tencent Holdings Limited</a>, which is well known for its other creations <a href="/wiki/Pengyou" title="Pengyou">Pengyou</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tencent_QQ" title="Tencent QQ">QQ</a>. As of  1&#160;October&#160;2012<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Soso_(search_engine)&amp;action=edit">&#91;update&#93;</a></sup>, Soso was ranked as the 33rd most visited website in the world, the 11th most visited website in China, and the number eight most visited website in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>, according to <a href="/wiki/Alexa_Internet" title="Alexa Internet">Alexa Internet</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In September 2013, Tencent invested in <a href="/wiki/Sogou" title="Sogou">Sogou</a>, a subsidiary of <a href="/wiki/Sohu" title="Sohu">Sohu</a>. At this point, Soso discontinued services and now redirects to Sogou Search.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>. Sogou also has search results in English which is powered by <a href="/wiki/Bing_(search_engine)" title="Bing (search engine)">Bing</a>.
</p>
