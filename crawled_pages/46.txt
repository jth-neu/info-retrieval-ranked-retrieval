<p><b>Parsijoo</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: پارسی‌جو) is an independent knowledge-base <a href="/wiki/Dot-com_company" title="Dot-com company">Internet company</a>, operating as a <a href="/wiki/Search_engine" class="mw-redirect" title="Search engine">search engine</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian language</a>. As of 2016, Parsijoo had 600,000 hits and 120,000 searches per day.<sup id="cite_ref-payvand.com_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-payvand.com-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Parsijoo is Iran's second most visited search engine after <a href="/wiki/Google" title="Google">Google</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-payvand.com_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-payvand.com-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parsijoo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The study phase of the project was started in 2001 and the first version of Parsijoo was released on the web in 2010 with just one service: web search. but now, it is providing more than is various online services which are currently running on fifth edition<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> (including image, music and news service). Parsijoo’s design is primarily based on the <a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian language</a> and culture.
</p><p>Each service has its own goal to help users find what they look for in the vast information available on the web.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Services">Services</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parsijoo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Services">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Web<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> - Quick and precise search among the Persian pages.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Image<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<ul><li>Video<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<ul><li>Ava<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> - search through songs, music tracks and audio files, and play and download directly</li></ul>
<ul><li>News<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> - the most intelligent Iranian news search service that has crawled news pages from more than 60 news website and provide the news to users with categorizing and intelligent grouping.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Market<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> - Market service has crawled different products from more than 20 online shopping reputable websites, and the product specifications (price, inventory, features), as well as the final purchase page provide to the user directly via a search.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Map<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> - Parsijoo map service is known as the first and most specialized native map service in Iran. Parsijoo map service has so important features such as wide coverage, precise details, draw ability, edit, location register (add a place), unique cartography, various information layers and multiple updates at short time intervals. There are currently about 650 cities of Iran with the coverage of 80% with more than 530 layers of information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Download<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> - Download service has crawled some various downloaded files (software, Android apps, books, movies, animation, etc.) of more than 20 respected and authorized download websites, and then the final files provide to the user directly via a search. The ability to categorize and grouping, as well as remove duplicates is in this service.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Translate<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> - powered by Targoman translate system and translates the text or word in real-time and online mode.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Parsijoo_Shakhes">Parsijoo Shakhes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parsijoo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Parsijoo Shakhes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Parsijoo Shakhes<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> is the first and only analyst of persian web pages. It provides quantitative and qualitative information about the status of Persian web pages on Farsi web.(e.g. Alexa) .
</p><p>Shakhes is able to display the number of crawled and indexed pages of a site, and then show the visibility mode of the site in Parsijoo search engine. Using the Shakhes, will be able to observe the number (and name) of sites that have links to a website, the number of clicks made on the site and also website ratings.
</p>
