<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Ahmia</h1>			<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
				<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>				<div id="contentSub"></div>
				<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Ahmia</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Clearnet_(networking)" title="Clearnet (networking)">clearnet</a> <a href="/wiki/Search_engine" class="mw-redirect" title="Search engine">search engine</a> for <a href="/wiki/Tor_(anonymity_network)" title="Tor (anonymity network)">Tor's hidden services</a> created by Juha Nurmi.
</p><p>Developed during the <a href="/wiki/Google_Summer_of_Code#2014" title="Google Summer of Code">2014 Google Summer of Code</a> with support from the <a href="/wiki/The_Tor_Project,_Inc" title="The Tor Project, Inc">Tor Project</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Open_source" class="mw-redirect" title="Open source">open source</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> search engine was initially built in <a href="/wiki/Django_(web_framework)" title="Django (web framework)">Django</a> and <a href="/wiki/PostgreSQL" title="PostgreSQL">PostgreSQL</a>. The site filters out <a href="/wiki/Child_pornography" title="Child pornography">child pornography</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The service partners with <a href="/wiki/GlobaLeaks" title="GlobaLeaks">GlobaLeaks</a>'s submissions and <a href="/wiki/Tor2web" title="Tor2web">Tor2web</a> statistics for hidden service discovery<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and as of July 2015 has indexed about 5000 sites.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In July 2015 the site published a list  of hundreds of fraudulent fake versions of web pages (including such sites as <a href="/wiki/DuckDuckGo" title="DuckDuckGo">DuckDuckGo</a>, as well a <a href="/wiki/Dark_web" title="Dark web">dark web</a> page).<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
