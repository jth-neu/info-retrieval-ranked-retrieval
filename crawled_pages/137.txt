<p><b>SAPO</b> (<a href="/wiki/Portuguese_language" title="Portuguese language">Portuguese</a> for <a href="/wiki/Toad" title="Toad">toad</a>), <b>S</b>ervidor de <b>A</b>pontadores <b>P</b>ortugueses <b>O</b>nline (Online Portuguese Links Server), is a <a href="/wiki/Brand" title="Brand">brand</a> and <a href="/wiki/Subsidiary_company" class="mw-redirect" title="Subsidiary company">subsidiary company</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Portugal_Telecom" class="mw-redirect" title="Portugal Telecom">Portugal Telecom</a> Group. It is a <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portuguese</a> <a href="/wiki/Internet_service_provider" title="Internet service provider">Internet service provider</a> that started as a <a href="/wiki/Search_engine" class="mw-redirect" title="Search engine">search engine</a> when founded in 1995. Currently, their search engine is powered by <a href="/wiki/Google_Search" title="Google Search">Google</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=SAPO_(company)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>SAPO was created on 4 September 1995 by the Computer Science Centre of the University of Aveiro. The name was derived from the <a href="/wiki/Acronym" title="Acronym">acronym</a> of the service, S.A.P.O. (Servidor de Apontadores Portugueses Online). This acronym corresponds to the Portuguese word for <a href="/wiki/Toad" title="Toad">toad</a>.
</p><p>In 1997 SAPO was converted in a business and the company Navegante was created.
</p><p>Later, in September 1998, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Saber_%26_Lazer&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Saber &amp; Lazer (page does not exist)">Saber &amp; Lazer</a> - Informática e Comunicação S.A. bought SAPO from Navegante. With Saber &amp; Lazer, SAPO launched new services; free <a href="/wiki/E-mail" class="mw-redirect" title="E-mail">e-mail</a>, a virtual shopping and some new features for the search engine.
</p><p>Still in that year, due to increasing traffic SAPO and <a href="/wiki/Telepac" title="Telepac">Telepac</a> signed an agreement, with 'Telepac' becoming their new <a href="/wiki/Internet_service_provider" title="Internet service provider">internet service provider</a>.
</p><p>In September 1999, <a href="/wiki/PT_Multim%C3%A9dia" class="mw-redirect" title="PT Multimédia">PT Multimédia</a> acquired 74.9% of Saber e Lazer. And in March 2000, SAPO was assigned to PTM.com, with the objective of joining all internet projects under only one company.
</p><p>After some improvements in infrastructures and accesses, finally in June 2002 the <a href="/wiki/ADSL" class="mw-redirect" title="ADSL">ADSL</a> access service was launched, starting the era of new contents for the <a href="/wiki/Web_portal" title="Web portal">portal</a>.
</p><p>On 28 March 2006, SAPO XL was launched: a project for <a href="/wiki/Broadband" title="Broadband">broadband</a> content, in which the main content is videos, on-line television transmission and real-time transmission of events.
</p><p>On 15 September 2010, SAPO launched its website in East Timor.
</p>
